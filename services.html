How It Works

Three automations. Zero effort from you.

We set up everything, customize it to your business, and you start seeing results within 48 hours.

01
Missed Call Text-Back

Never lose a caller again.

When you miss a call, our system sends a personalized text within 5 seconds. The customer thinks YOU texted them. A conversation starts, and you respond when you're free.

✓ Instant text-back in under 5 seconds
✓ Customized with your business name and tone
✓ Works 24/7 including nights and weekends
✓ Conversation flows to your phone via text
✓ After-hours messages with custom responses
Result: Businesses recover up to 40% of leads they were previously losing.

02
Smart Follow-Up Sequences

Every lead gets followed up. Automatically.

When a new lead comes in -- from a form, a call, or a text -- they enter an automated follow-up sequence. They get a text right away, an email the next morning, and another check-in on day 3. The sequence runs until they book or reply.

✓ Multi-channel: SMS + email follow-ups
✓ Timed sequences over days/weeks
✓ Stops automatically when they reply or book
✓ Custom messages for your industry
✓ Re-engagement campaigns for old leads
Result: 3-5x more appointments booked compared to manual follow-up.
Instant SMS: "Thanks for reaching out! When's a good time to chat?"
Day 1 - 9am Email: Follow-up with service info + booking link
Day 3 SMS: "Still interested? We have openings this week!"
Day 7 SMS: Final check-in + special offer
03
Automated Review Requests

Turn every happy customer into a 5-star review.

After every appointment, your customer gets a friendly text with a direct link to your Google Reviews page. No awkward asking. No manual work. Just a steady stream of authentic reviews building your online reputation.

✓ Automated text after each appointment
✓ Direct link to your Google Reviews
✓ Follow-up reminder if they don't leave one
✓ Track review count growth on your dashboard
✓ Negative feedback routed privately to you first
Result: Average of 3x more Google reviews per month.
